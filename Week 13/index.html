HEy there! 

I'm Gracie! I'm a girl in my 20s, who loves Jesus, and is from the middle of nowhere Nebraska. Something I love the most are horses, I've spent most of my life on them, and as clumsy as I can be sometimes, I think I can ride better than I can walk! I also love to travel and experience different cultures! I am an overcomer that loves using my creativity to serve and connect with others. I have big dreams to make an impact on the world!

Five years ago I started a business journey helping others like me find their confidence and freedom! Now, five years and four businesses later...I've learned a lot and have refined what I offer down to a few select services providing high quality results and materials. When I meet with a new client I like to sit down and get to know them. I want to know your goals and what makes you...YOU! When we are done with your marketing materials it needs to ultimately reflect you. My job is to make you feel good on the inside, and look good on the outside!

I can't wait to connect with you and hear your story! We are all designed for greatness! Let's achieve your goals together!            

- That Gracie Girl
